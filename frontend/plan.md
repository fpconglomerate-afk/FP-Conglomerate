# FP Conglomerate Website Direction

## Brand Positioning
- Present FP as a high-end, institution-first corporate group.
- Emphasize quiet authority, long-term thinking, and operational depth.
- Prioritize clarity over decoration; fewer visual effects, stronger content hierarchy.

## Design Principles
- Increase vertical rhythm with generous section spacing and cleaner reading width.
- Use editorial typography for major headlines and restrained sans-serif body copy.
- Reduce visual clutter: fewer competing accents, consistent border and panel language.
- Introduce controlled asymmetry through offset grids and staggered section blocks.
- Make each section visually distinct with subtle tonal transitions and spacing shifts.

## Structure and UX
- Keep global navigation simple and page-first (`Home`, `Blog`, `Contact`).
- Ensure each routed page can stand on its own with its own strong hero/introduction.
- Improve section-to-section flow using background transitions, not heavy graphics.
- Keep CTAs concise and deliberate.
